# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, including creators, artists, designers, developers, operators, and users, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Scope

This code applies to this project's issues, pull requests, discussions, security reports, maintainer communication, and shared support spaces. It also applies when someone represents this project in public community settings.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints, workflows, creative practices, and deployment constraints.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for this project's users and maintainers.
- Respecting creative ownership, private prompts, private reference media, generated media, deployment details, and operational constraints.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery in community spaces.
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information, prompts, reference media, generated media, deployment URLs, logs, or credentials without explicit permission.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors to disclose secrets, private customer data, unreleased vulnerability details, or private generated media.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at `dev@babysea.ai`. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, block accounts, or take other reasonable action when behavior does not meet this code of conduct.

Reports that include secrets, private prompts, private reference media, generated media, vulnerability details, or deployment details should be sent privately instead of posted in public project spaces. For security-sensitive reports, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
